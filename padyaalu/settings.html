Goals లక్ష్యాలు

Vision

A clean, faithful, dependency-free digital home for Telugu śataka literature — every padyam on its own full-page card with Telugu meaning and a live prosody breakdown.

Short-term

Finish Dāśarathi: verify chandassu for the remaining verses, reconcile the few supplied lines whose syllable counts are off, and author Telugu prati padārtham + tātparyam for all 104.

Long-term

Grow to many śatakams (Bhāskara, Kṛṣṇa, Kumārī, Nārāyaṇa…), complete Vemana & Sumati to their full ~100+ verses, and add search, transliteration, audio recitation, and deeper prosody (yati/prāsa) checks.
