🌲 Forest Friends
Only A animals now — say “Ant enter” or “3 Ant enter” — a new letter follows each time · “exit all” anytime

🌳 How to play

  1. Tap Tap to talk and allow the microphone.
  2. A letter shows in the bottom-right. You may only call the animal for that letter — the bottom row lists them.
  3. Say the animal and “come”: “Bear enter”, “Bear come”, or in Telugu “Bear raa” / “ఎలుగుబంటి రా”.
  4. It runs, flies, or swims in with its voice (heard three times!) and roams the whole forest — birds in the sky, fish in the pond, animals on the ground — then a new letter pops up. 🐻🐦🐠
  5. Want a whole family at once? Say a number: “3 lions enter” (up to five) — they all roam together! 🦁🦁🦁
  6. Tap any animal to hear its sound again. 🔊
  7. Send some home: “Lion exit” (one) or “5 lion exit” (up to five). po / bye work too.
  8. Special commands anytime: “exit all animals” empties the forest; “reduce zoo size” randomly sends some home.
  9. No microphone? Tap an animal in the bottom row to summon it. Use 🎲 New letter to skip a letter.

Tip: works best in Chrome. Everything runs offline — the animals are emoji and every sound is made live in your browser (no downloads).
