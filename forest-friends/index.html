🌲 Forest Friends
Animals for A — say “Ant raa” or “Ant enter”

🌳 How to play

  1. Tap Tap to talk and allow the microphone.
  2. A letter appears in the bottom-right. The bottom row lists animals for that letter.
  3. Say an animal and “come”: “Bear enter”, “Bear come”, or in Telugu “Bear raa” / “ఎలుగుబంటి రా”.
  4. The animal walks in making its own sound! 🐻🔊
  5. Say any animal you like — not only the letter's. To send one home say “Lion go”, “Lion po”, or “Lion bye”.
  6. No microphone? Just tap an animal in the bottom row to bring it in.

Tip: works best in Chrome. Everything runs offline — the animals are emoji and every sound is made live in your browser (no downloads).
